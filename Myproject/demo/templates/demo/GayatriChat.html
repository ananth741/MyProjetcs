Vision of founder

Gayathri Catering Services was founded by Mr. Sitaramaiah Kurella in the year 1990. He spent 14 years working with his uncle before starting his own brand that became Hyderabad’s iconic food Catering Services Company.

Mr Sitaramaiah Kurella is a vastly experienced caterer and wedding planner in Hyderabad. His great vision and energy gave the company its direction for where it is placed today. With his sheer determination and passion he has built up his army of never resting soldiers and made sure that Gayathri Catering Services becomes the most customer centric company and gives the highest customer satisfaction in Southern states.
